THESISPM‑002
One sentence, three consequences

Most teams will ship a document chatbot. We are building the opposite.

01 — GRAPH-FIRST

The knowledge graph is the product. The LLM is the interface over it. Retrieval finds documents; traversal finds connections no human can hold in their head. Linkage is on the rubric — vectors in a blob are not.

02 — PUSH, NOT PULL

The system speaks first, unprompted, when the graph detects a condition worth attention — before anyone thinks to ask. This is where the innovation points live.

03 — REFUSAL IS A FEATURE

A confidently wrong answer can kill someone. When the graph lacks an answer, the system says so and names the human who knows — visibly, not hidden.

ARCHITECTUREPM‑003
Corpus to control room

Rules do the reasoning. The model only writes.

Four hardcoded Cypher‑style traversals detect conditions; the LLM narrates what the traversal already proved. That inversion is why alerts stay reliable while a raw RAG chatbot would hallucinate.
