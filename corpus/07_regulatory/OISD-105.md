# OISD-105: Fire Protection Facilities for Petroleum Refineries and Oil/Gas Processing Plants (representative)

### OISD-105-4.2 (p.12)

Positive isolation and verification of flush/cooling flow shall be confirmed before startup of rotating equipment handling flammable hydrocarbons.

### OISD-105-6.1 (p.18)

Fixed fire detection shall be provided in areas handling Class A/B petroleum products above defined inventory thresholds.
