# OISD-192: Confined Space Entry and Vessel Isolation (representative)

### OISD-192-2.1 (p.4)

Vessel entry shall not proceed until isolation, purging, and atmosphere testing are independently verified and logged.

### OISD-192-3.6 (p.7)

Level and pressure instrumentation bridles on hydrocarbon-service vessels shall be maintained free of blockage; any recurring fouling behaviour shall be documented in the equipment's governing procedure, not left to informal practice.
