### OISD-116-HYDRANT-SPACING (p.22)

In high hazard areas, at least one hydrant post shall be provided for every 30 metres of external wall measurement or unit battery limit perimeter; hydrants protecting utilities and miscellaneous buildings in high hazard areas may be spaced at 45 metre intervals.

### OISD-116-HYDRANT-DISTANCE (p.23)

Hydrants shall be located at a minimum distance of 15 metres from the periphery of the storage tank or hazardous equipment under protection, with horizontal hose-connection coverage not considered beyond 45 metres.
